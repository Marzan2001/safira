About Me

I am a CSE student of MIST. From the very beginning of my puberty,I was interested in hijabs and its varieties. I always dreamt of having my own hijab store where I'll be importing finest hijabs and make my clients happy.

For making my dream true,I made this website. I may not start my own hijab store now,but I can at least make a website by now and in sha Allah one day I'll make my own hijab chain. In this page I mostly tried to merge all my favourite types of hijab and which I always wear on daily basis and on party also. Hijab fabric is very important so my priority will always be the fabric and its finest works.
